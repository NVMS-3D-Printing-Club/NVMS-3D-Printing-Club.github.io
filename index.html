FAQ

Who can join?

Any student interested in 3D printing is welcome, but all of the crew will have to agree on you joining the club, and how you perform during training.

Do I need experience?

No experience is required. We'll teach everything from the basics.

How do I sign up?

Fill out the form in the contact section below.

Are you taking printing requests right now?

At the moment not yet, we are focusing on setting up first, but you may submit orders at this time.
